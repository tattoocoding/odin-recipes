Lasagna

Chef Boyardee Be Damned

Ingredients:
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Ricotta
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Sheets
Steps:
  1. Cook a meat ragu.
  2. Mix ricotta with herbs, egg, and parm.
  3. Coat the bottom of a caserlie with ragu.
  4. Layer lasagna sheets, then ricotta and ragu interchangably.
  5. Add a final layer of parmesean to the top.
  6. Bake covered at 350 until noodles are cooked.
  7. Remove cover and broil until cheese on top is melted.
