Pie

"This Pie Is Just OK"

Ingredients:
  • Flour
  • Butter
  • Forraged Fruits
  • Spices
  • A little bit of love
Steps:
  1. Form a shaggy dough by mixing together flour and butter.
  2. Form a crust in a baking dish by pressing the dough in firmly.
  3. Make sure your forraged fruits aren't poisonous.
  4. If you're still alive, put those into the pie shell.
  5. Under a full moon, sing a babylonian chant.
  6. Bake covered at 350 until berries are bubbling.
  7. Serve to your local love interest.
